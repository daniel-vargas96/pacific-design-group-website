Financial

Pacific Design Group has worked extensively in the Retail Banking, Personal Banking and Commercial Banking sectors, with clients ranging from well established Fortune 500 institutions to small start up banks. PDG's attention to personal service, experience and network with contracting and building professionals are assets key to the timely and successful completion of all of our retail banking projects.

Services provided range from preliminary site and building evaluation, to space planning, architectural design, construction drawings and construction administration. Some organizations already have well established design criteria, others may desire assistance in analyzing or further developing their criteria. In some cases, PDG has assisted start-up banks with developing a distinguishing retail design theme that can be followed through on future branches. In other cases, PDG has assisted in re-packaging the bank's image throughout all of a company's existing retail branches.
PDG's knowledge of banking operations, equipment and security systems assures that we will apply our experience throughout the process from design through construction and be able to coordinate with all trades for a successful and timely project delivery.
