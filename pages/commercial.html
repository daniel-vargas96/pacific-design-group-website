Commercial

One of Pacific Design Group's core businesses is providing architectural services to commercial clients throughout California. These services include building and site evaluation, space planning, programming, real estate asset planning construction drawings and construction administration. Projects range is size from 1,500 square foot tenant improvements to corporate campus planning and design for 400,000 square feet. Following are some of the services PDG provides:

Building/ Site Evaluation
Evaluate building and site suitability for a given use, including determination of city entitlement processes, preliminary site planning, and parking analyses.

Real Estate Asset Planning
Update on a monthly basis color floor plans which indicate all departments and personnel locations and areas for entire corporate campuses. This data is a valuable tool used by corporate facility managers for growth management, space planning and rent allocation.

Architectural and Engineering Design
PDG works closely with each client to understand space needs, interdepartmental adjacency requirements and corporate standards. These requirements are document in program form and used to proceed with space planning options, and drawings for construction and permitting.

Construction Administration
PDG works closely with the owner and contractor during construction to verify contractor payment requests, observe construction progress, and produce as-built drawings. Critical to this process is experience in the field, and ability to problem solve and provide timely responses to contractor inquiries.
