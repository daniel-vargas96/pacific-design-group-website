About

Pacific Design Group is a broad-based design practice consisting of several design practices; master planning, High-Tech, Healthcare, Financial and Mixed-use. Each of these practices employ professionals with respective areas of expertise. These practices cross-collaborate and share knowledge that adds to the success of projects large and small.

At PDG, we take great pride in our ability to devote a great deal of personal attention to each and every one of our clients; each receiving informed answers and superior results throughout the project life cycle. As a result, these same clients continue to entrust us with their projects time and again. Today, over 70% of our projects are from our long-term and repeat clients; a statistic that we are very proud of.

Established in 1995, PDG has offices in Orange County and Los Angeles County and has completed project throughout the Western United States, New York, New Jersey, Canada and China. Our success lies in our ability to help clients establish their needs and priorities and then to translate those needs into creative solutions and workable schedules and budgets. The key to PDG's quality design is its experienced and dedicated staff. PDG staff brings a high level of experience and complementary skills to our clients, providing the confidence to undertake the most complex challenges. Our principals and staff provide an unparalleled degree of responsiveness and personal service to each and every client.
